Hidden Gems of Nueva Ecija

Let’s not sugarcoat it, Nueva Ecija is known for its expansive rice fields, not exactly for its tourism. And in reality, the province is not on people’s bucket lists for places to visit in the Philippines. But as author Ronald Dahl said, “the greatest secrets are always hidden in the most unlikely places.” Nueva Ecija is full of hidden gems for anyone looking to reconnect with nature, take in a rich history, or to delve into spectacular cuisine.
